## 1. Introduction

The application Trip To The Zoo is developed and published by Kids Games Projects.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the data of all users, especially children.
This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR),
Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

The app is primarily designed for children aged 9 and above, but it may also be used by adults.

---

## 2. Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect, store, or process any personal data such as name, email address,
phone number, or identifiers of any kind.
No user registration, login, or account creation is required.

The application does not display advertisements and does not use any advertising networks.

The only data that may be processed are minimal technical, non-identifiable device data,
strictly required for the correct technical operation of the app and its stability.
These data are handled automatically by the underlying platform and game engine.

Such technical data may include:
- device type and model
- operating system version
- app version
- anonymous crash or error logs

This information is processed in an anonymous and aggregated form
and cannot be used to identify any user.

The app does not collect or use:
- location data (GPS)
- camera or microphone access
- contacts, photos, or files
- any form of analytics related to user behavior

---

## 3. Unity Services

The app is developed using the Unity engine.
Unity may automatically process limited technical data such as device type,
platform, and crash diagnostics to ensure application stability and performance.

No personally identifiable data are collected, stored, or accessed by the developer.

For more information, please refer to Unity’s privacy policy:
https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

## 4. Children’s Privacy

Our app is designed for children and fully complies with COPPA and GDPR-K
(Children’s Data Protection) regulations.
We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13
(or under 16 in the European Union).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that any personal data may have been
unintentionally processed, please contact us at:
kidsgameprojects@gmail.com
