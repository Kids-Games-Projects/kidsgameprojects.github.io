## 1. Introduction

The application **Bilingual Kids Stories** is developed and published by **Kids Games Projects**.  
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the data of all users, especially children.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in accordance with:

## 2. Information Collection and Use

The app **does not collect or store any personal data** such as name, email, or phone number.  
No user registration or account creation is required.

The app may process limited **technical, non-identifiable data** through third-party services for:
- app functionality,
- advertising,
- purchase validation.

These data may include:
- device type and model,  
- operating system version,  
- app version,  
- IP address (used temporarily),  
- language and region settings,  
- anonymous usage statistics,  
- ad performance data (impressions, clicks).

All such data are:
- processed in an **anonymous and aggregated form**,  
- **not used for profiling**,  
- **not used for personalized advertising for children**.

The app does **not** access:
- precise location (GPS),  
- camera, microphone, contacts,  
- files stored on the device.

## 3. Advertising

The app uses **Google AdMob** to display **non-personalized ads**.

The app is treated as **child-directed content** in accordance with COPPA and Google’s policies.

- Ads are non-personalized  
- Behavioral targeting is disabled  
- Ads are filtered to be appropriate for children  

More information:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy
